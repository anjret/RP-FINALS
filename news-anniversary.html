The Arellano 86th Founding Anniversary
After three pandemic years, Arellano University celebrates its 86th Founding Anniversary with a bang!

Different clubs and organizations from the seven campuses of the university contributed well-prepared presentations, mostly in the main campus, and others in their respective campuses. The opening of the 86th Founding Anniversary was declared by Dr. Aurora A. Franco, Director of Basic Education. The participation of the elementary, high school and college departments were assured by the following personalities: Ms. May V. Eliano, Principal of the Elementary Department; Mr. Mark C. Malonzo, Principal of the High School Department, and AVP Abegail H. Cayco of the Marketing Department. One of the highlights of the anniversary celebration was the grand alumni homecoming, where graduates from different batches reunited to reminisce about their fond memories of Arellano and reconnect with old friends and mentors.
